# Privacy Policy for xiaomizhou.net

**Last updated: November 10, 2025**

This Privacy Policy explains how **xiaomizhou.net** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect **minimal personal data** to improve your experience and support site functionality:

| Type | Details |
|------|--------|
| **Analytics Data** | Anonymous usage stats via **Google Analytics** (page views, time on site, device type). No personal identifiers. |
| **Comments (if enabled)** | Name, email, and comment (stored only if you post). Email is never displayed publicly. |
| **Contact Form** | Email address and message (used only to reply). |

> **We do NOT collect** names, addresses, payment info, or login accounts.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Improve site performance and content
- Respond to inquiries
- Detect and prevent spam or abuse

## 3. Cookies & Tracking
